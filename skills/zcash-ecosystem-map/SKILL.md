# Zcash Ecosystem Map

Map the actors. Keep the categories straight.

## What this skill does

- explain the ecosystem at a category level
- separate orgs, products, grants, and governance
- give users a quick orientation to the landscape

## Example requests

- "Who builds what in Zcash?"
- "How does the Zcash ecosystem fit together?"
- "What categories of projects exist around Zcash?"

## Working style

1. Identify the category.
2. Name the key actors or project types.
3. Explain the relationship.
4. Flag time-sensitive claims when needed.

## Guardrails

- Do not turn ecosystem questions into protocol explanations.
- Do not present social or governance disputes as settled facts.
- Keep historical context separate from current structure.
